Bivariate spline fit for interpolation purposes

Below is the bivariate spline fit formed using the parameters width and equivalent perimeter on area. For ease of use the results are plotted with respect to length and width. The valid fitting region is given as a colourwash. The colour of the colourwash is representative of the fitted cutout factor.

Hovering your mouse over the righthand plot will give the cutout factor results in the z parameter. Where the z paramater returns null, and there is no colour wash, is not a valid fitting region. This may be due either to not having enough points in the direct vicinity, or the region being outside the external data boundary.

Hovering your mouse over the lefthand plot will provide you with a way to lookup individual measured data points. Your measured data is given in blue. The equivalent shape with width and length inverted is given in orange.
